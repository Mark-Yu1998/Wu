    Why Wu Zetian?

    She is the one and only female emperor in Chinese history, she is a woman who been through a lot to achieve the throne. Such as she lost her sons, her friends, and her husbands, yes two of them. It is remarkable that she started as a candidate concubine of the emperor Li Shimin and ended up becoming the one and only female emperor in Chinese. In ancient China, women had very low social status, they were not allowed to go to school, and they could not leave their house until the day of their wedding. All they could learn were skills to help their future husband and children.
